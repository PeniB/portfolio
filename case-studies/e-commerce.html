Case Study

Teacher Resource E-Commerce Ecosystem

WordPress • WooCommerce • Hostinger • Digital Products

Overview

A niche e-commerce system designed specifically for educators, structured around how teachers search, select, and use classroom materials. The platform prioritizes speed, clarity, and organized discovery.

Problem Statement

Teachers currently face:
  • Fragmented resource platforms
  • Inconsistent content quality
  • Poor category alignment (grade/subject confusion)
  • Time-heavy lesson preparation

Project Goals

Easy Discovery

Find resources quickly by grade & subject

Structured System

Clear taxonomy for all resources

Fast Checkout

Minimal steps to purchase & download

Solution Approach

Information Architecture

Products structured by grade, subject, and resource type for intuitive discovery.

WooCommerce Build

Automated digital delivery with optimized product pages.

User Experience

Mobile-first, clean layout with minimal friction browsing.
