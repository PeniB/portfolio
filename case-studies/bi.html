School Financial BI Dashboard

Power BI • Financial Tracking • Ledger Analytics

Building a Financial Tracking & Payment Monitoring System for a School

This project focused on transforming manual receipt-book records into an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard capable of tracking school payments, monitoring balances, and simplifying operational reporting.

The goal was to improve financial transparency, reduce manual reconciliation work, and help administrators quickly identify debtors, payment trends, and category-specific collections.

Project Overview

Power BI Reporting

Interactive dashboards and financial summaries for school operations.

Ledger Tracking

Structured tracking of receipt-book transactions and balances.

Debtor Analysis

Quick identification of outstanding balances and unpaid accounts.

Payment Insights

Analysis of fees, meals, boarding, uniforms, and requirements.

The Challenge

The school relied heavily on manual receipt books and spreadsheet summaries, making financial tracking time-consuming and difficult to maintain consistently.

  • Tracking Difficulty monitoring payment progress across students.
  • Debtors Delayed identification of unpaid balances.
  • Verification Limited visibility into issued items like uniforms and requirements.
  • Reporting Manual reconciliation processes consumed significant time.

BI Solution

Power BI data transformation workflow showing cleaning, restructuring, and preparation of raw operational data.

Interactive Power BI ledger report providing financial tracking and transaction visibility.

Structured Excel matrix report exported from Power BI for operational analysis and reporting.

This reporting system transformed raw operational data into structured, actionable insights through automated transformation workflows and interactive dashboards. By combining Power BI reporting with export-ready Excel summaries, the solution improved visibility, reduced manual reporting effort, and supported faster decision-making across financial and operational processes.
